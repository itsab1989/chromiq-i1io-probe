ChromIQ - i1iO information collector
====================================

Thank you for helping!  This takes about two minutes.


THE SHORT VERSION
-----------------

1. Plug in your i1iO and switch it on.

2. Double-click the file for your system:

       macOS    ->  Start-macOS.command
       Windows  ->  Start-Windows.bat
       Linux    ->  Start-Linux.sh

3. Follow what it says on screen.  It asks you three easy questions
   at the end - you may skip them.

4. It saves a text file called  chromiq-io-probe-<date>.txt
   Have a look at it, then attach it to:

       https://github.com/itsab1989/ChromIQ/issues/129

That's it.  You're done.


IS IT SAFE?
-----------

Yes.  It never sends anything to the table and it CANNOT move the arm.
It only reads information your computer already has about plugged-in
hardware.  Nothing will move.  Nothing is changed.


IF YOUR COMPUTER REFUSES TO OPEN IT
-----------------------------------

That is normal for downloaded files and does not mean anything is wrong.

   macOS  :  RIGHT-CLICK the file, choose "Open", then "Open" again.
   Windows:  Click "More info", then "Run anyway".
   Linux  :  Open a terminal here and run:  bash Start-Linux.sh


MORE DETAIL
-----------

README.md answers everything else - what it collects, what it does
about your privacy, what to do if it goes wrong, and an optional
advanced step you are very welcome to skip.
